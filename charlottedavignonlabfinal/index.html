My Hometown

Lakeville, Minnesota

Lakeville sits south of the Twin Cities and feels like a mix of lakes, trails, schools, local businesses, and neighborhoods that keep growing while still feeling close to nature.

Explore places Plan a day

Why Lakeville Stands Out

Lakeville is one of those suburbs where everyday life is connected to outdoor space. The city has lakes, community parks, sports fields, trails, and neighborhood gathering places that make it easy to spend time outside.

Quick Facts

  • Located in Dakota County, Minnesota.
  • Population estimate: 77,971 in 2024, according to the U.S. Census Bureau.
  • About 36 square miles of land area.
  • Home to a large parks and trails system.

A hometown with room to move

The City of Lakeville lists 67 parks, 49 playgrounds, 20 conservation areas, and about 149 miles of trails and sidewalks. That is a big part of what gives the city its active, family-friendly personality.
